Targol Khalighi

(Professional Taar, Rubab, Setar Player) Founder and Supervisor of Tida Ensemble | Born in 1988
Education:
Master of Music(Tehran University of Art)
Graduated from Girls' Music Conservatory
Studied Taar under supervision of Houshang Zarif, Shahriar Faryousefi, Mahmoud Behrouz and Mohsen Nafar
Career:
Has played alongside very well-known Iranian vocalists and musicians internationally such as:
Homayoun Shajarian and Hamid Motebassem in Simorgh Ensemble for 5 years including Europe concert tour (Netherland, Germany and etc.)
Alireza Ghorbani in Eshtiagh Ensemble for 6 years all around Iran, Frankfurt and etc.
Ardeshir Kamkar
Bijan Kamkar
Behzad Abdi in Khonyagaran Europe concert in Germany,France,Swiss and Womex festival 2019 in Finland .
Mohammad Motamedi in Mehr Ensemble
Mahyar Alizadeh in Tehran Ensemble
National Orchestra of Iran for 5 years in Kuwait, China, Berlin, Italy (Milan Festival Beat), Swiss and etc.
Alireza Assar
Reza sadeghi
Mahtab Ensemble
Paleez Ensemble
Has performed in International Music festivals as a solo and group player Such as Tida Group
Has been teaching music for 10 years
Played in Movies music such as: Pardeh Neshin, Mostanad Iran, Zaferani, Shahr Moshha and etc.
Achievements:
The Simorq Album: Homayoun Shajarian and Hamid Motebassem
The in The Mirror of Delusions Album in collaboration with Hesam Inanlou. (Rubab & Kamancheh)
The Gahi Segahi Album: Mohamad Motamedi
The Abru Kaman: Mohamad Motamedi, Ehsan Zabihi far The Dancing in the flames with you: Mahyar Alizadeh The Hich Magu: Mojtaba Asgari
The An Roozha:Vahid Taaj, Fereydoon Poorreza
The Beshno Az No: Bahram Dehghanyar
Video music album , Khanyagarane Mehr.
